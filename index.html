Research

research

In the human modeling group, through technologies that extend human exercise and sensation, we aim to realize an excellent human mechanical system. In order to enjoy the daily life even at the age, we think that it is important to maintain the feeling that I am moving my body freely by myself, and feel a sense of feeling various things by myself. Human Modeling Group research is focused in 3 main areas: Assistive technology, haptics and interface.

Assistive

In order to enjoy the daily life even at the age, I feel that it is important to maintain my sense of being able to move my body exactly and maintain a feeling of various things by myself. Therefore, we are developing a human extended assist device that expands/improves human's exercise and sensory functions.

Haptics

With the evolution of computer technology, it is becoming possible to reproduce the physiological, mechanistic and mechanical properties of muscles and skeletons with computers. By applying these body modeling techniques, we evaluate by assessing the characteristics of human exercise and sensation with a clear indicator of the physical grounds, and then use auxiliary technologies to support people more intelligently and more product design We are studying the method to connect to.

Interface

Developing new technologies for telepresence and coaching.
