Soft exoskeletons

Soft exoskeletons are a branch of technology derivate from hard exoskeletons, these devices were developed to provide an alternative to main problems of exoskeletons: an exoskeleton is a hard, rigid structure that must be worn over a soft, flexible body. Soft exoskeletons and exosuits do away with ALL the hard, rigid frames that make up a classical exoskeleton. This new wearable dispositive is made entirely out of soft materials. Some components, such as battery packs and controllers must remain rigid, but are usually stashed away in a backpack behind the user.
